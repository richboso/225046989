Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to complete a website?

The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the project. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, The timeline varies depending on the complexity of the project. A simple website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while more complex projects can take 6-12 weeks. I'll provide a detailed timeline during our initial consultation based on your specific requirements and desired launch date.

What information do you need to start a project?

To get started, I need to understand your goals, target audience, and design preferences. It's helpful if you can provide examples of websites you like, your brand guidelines (logos, colors, etc.), and content for your website. I'll guide you through this process with a comprehensive project questionnaire.

Do you provide content writing services?

While I focus primarily on design and development, I can recommend professional copywriters who specialize in creating compelling web content. Alternatively, I can work with content you provide. If you need help with basic content optimization for SEO purposes, that's included in my services.

How do we communicate during the project?

Communication is key to a successful project. We'll have regular check-ins via email, phone, or video calls based on your preference. I use project management tools that allow you to track progress, provide feedback, and approve milestones throughout the development process.

Do you offer website maintenance after launch?

Yes, I offer various maintenance packages to keep your website secure, up-to-date, and performing optimally. These packages can include regular updates, security monitoring, content updates, backup services, and technical support. We can discuss these options once your project is nearing completion.

What is your payment structure?

I typically work with a 50% upfront deposit to secure your project in my schedule, with the remaining balance due upon project completion. For larger projects, we can arrange milestone payments. I accept payments via direct bank transfer, PayPal, or credit card.
